Roomworld

Instructions


Welcome to Roomworld!



Play 92 short rounds. This will take you a total of 30 to 60 minutes to complete.



Each round is a set of rooms on a different floor. You are challenged to move through the rooms to get to the gift.

Use the arrow keys ➡ ⬇ ⬆ ⬅ on your keyboard to navigate the space.


If you're unsure how to solve a round, you can give up and skip to the next round.



Make sure to give it your full attention. Find a quiet place with a good internet connection and turn off your notifications.
