Roomworld

Instructions


Welcome to Roomworld!



Play 92 short rounds. This will take you a total of 30 to 60 minutes to complete.



Each round is a set of rooms on a different floor. You are challenged to move through the rooms to get to the gift. Every fifth round will be a Challenge Round, more difficult than the ones before.


Use the arrow keys ➡ ⬇ ⬆ ⬅ on your screen to navigate the space.


If you're unsure how to solve a round, you have two options:



  • You can try to solve the round again. To do this, press restart round. This will restart the same round. You can do this up to three times on a regular round, but can't do it on a Challenge Round.
  • You can also give up and skip to the next round. To do this, click skip round. This will take you to the next round.

Your first time playing is what counts, so make sure to give it your full attention. Closing the browser and relaunching the game will resume your progress.



Tips:
  • Find a quiet place with a good internet connection and turn off your notifications.
  • If you find that your connection isn't good enough, you can stop playing and come back later
